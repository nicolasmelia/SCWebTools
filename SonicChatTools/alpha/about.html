Let me introduce myself

SonicChat is an All-In-One, intuitive customer support widget that was designed to be easily implemented, simple to use, and extremely versatile. Customizing SonicChat to fit your needs is as easy as creating a post and tagging your friends on Facebook â€“

1. Tiles

Tiles are square clickable icons embedded directly into your SonicChat widget. When a Tile is clicked on, it opens specific support functionality in the chat box that your customers can interact with. Tiles give you the freedom to personalize your widget by offering many different Tile solutions that best fit your needs.

Tiles unique as your business -
  • Chat
  • Ticket
  • Appointment
  • AnswerBase
  • Message
  • Map
  • Many More

2. AnswerBase

Being an All-In-One system allows you to make a change once and have it work everywhere. The SonicChat AnswerBase offers a good example of this as it houses all of your product, service and company information. This system integrates into every aspect of your SonicChat experience, from your chat dashboard to specific tile functionalities. All-In-One means exactly that, one and done.

3. Easy Implementation

This Chat widget adheres your website with ONE simple line of html. This is designed to be efficient, easy to maintain, and user friendly. No messy files or multiple lines of code.
